## Chapter 1

### Exercise 1

Suppose \\(a\\) and \\(b\\) are real numbers, not both 0. Find real
numbers \\(c\\) and \\(d\\) such that

$$
    \frac{
        1
    }{
        a + bi
    }
    = c + di
$$

### Solution

$$

    \begin{split}
        1 &= (c + di) (a + bi)
        \\
        &= ca + adi + cbi + dbi^2
        \\
        &= ca + db + adi + cbi
        \\
        &= ca + db + (ad + cb)i
    \end{split}
$$
